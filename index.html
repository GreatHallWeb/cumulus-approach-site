About Cumulus Approach

Cumulus Approach creates simple aviation tools for general aviation pilots and CFIs. Our goal is to build approachable utilities that simplify everyday aviation tasks.

Our Apps

Quick aviation expiry calculations for pilots and CFIs.

Stop looking up future dates, just enter a date, choose a duration and get the answer instantly.

Cumulus Expiry helps pilots and CFIs quickly calculate aviation expiration dates for solo endorsements, flight reviews and more.

Who is Cumulus Expiry for?
Pilots, Student Pilots, CFIs or anyone else who needs a quick way to calculate common expiration dates.
Does Cumulus Expiry require an account?
No, the app does not need an account
Does the app have a home screen widget?
Yes, you can add the widget to your iPhone home screen to quickly get a date based on today's date. You can configure this in the app.
